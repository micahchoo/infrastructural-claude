# Conjunction Check

You already decompose scenarios into steps — that's not the risk. The risk is that a coherent narrative *feels* more probable than the math allows. A story where each step logically follows the last is seductive precisely because it's a good story.

After decomposing: assign each step a rough probability. Multiply them. Compare that product to your gut feeling about the whole scenario. If there's a gap — and there almost always is — trust the math. A five-step chain where each link is 50% likely has a 3% end-to-end probability, not the 40-60% that the narrative suggests.

`[eval: feasibility]` Compound probability <50% requires a fallback path.
